Our Parks

Explore our portfolio of theme and water parks across the country.

Astroverse Ohio Resort (Flagship Resort)

  • Astroverse Worlds of Adventure: A vibrant celebration of fantasy, sci-fi, and global adventure.
  • Astroverse Century Springs: Time-travel through four lands: New York Present Day, Detroit 1920, Hollywood 1980, Dodge City 1870.
  • Mardi Gras Splash: A lively water park inspired by New Orleans culture.
  • Astroverse Town Center: Shopping, dining, and entertainment hub.

Florida Property (St. Augustine, FL)

Florida Adventure Park: Combining Florida’s natural wonders with thrilling storytelling and attractions.

Former Germanator Enterprises Parks

  • Germanator Studios New York: East Coast studio park with behind-the-scenes thrills.
  • Germanator Studios Los Angeles: Immersive studio experiences and West Coast entertainment.
  • Germanator Bay (Water Park): Cinematic slides and wave lagoon, connected to Germanator Studios Los Angeles.
  • Germanator Studios Daytona Beach: Racing heritage meets film and coastal culture.
  • Red Rock River Water Park: Water park with red rock canyon theming, connected to Germanator Studios Daytona Beach.
  • Mountain Park: Forested mountain adventure and thrill rides.
  • Marshland: Haunted swamp-themed park for supernatural lore fans.
  • Germanator Gardens (Under Construction): Botanical-themed park celebrating seasons and festivals.
  • Oakmont Park: Family-friendly Americana with nostalgic charm.
  • Germanator Beach: Oceanside amusement park with classic boardwalk vibes.
